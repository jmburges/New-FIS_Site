A 12 Week Intensive Program in New York City

  • Learn to Code

    What graduates grok

    The Flatiron school is a 12 week, full time program, designed to turn you into a web developer. Prior to arriving to campus, you will complete 4 weeks (80-100 hours in total) of prework assignments at home. This, plus the following 12 weeks of immersive campus learning, amounts to a 16 week commitment.

    Learn More →

  • Become a Developer

    What we look for

    The goal of the program is to give you the equivalent skillset and experience of an entry-level Ruby developer. That means you'll be an internet swiss-army knife, equipped with all the tools needed to build applications. You don't need any programming experience to attend, just a strong desire to create and a whole lot of grit.

    Apply Now →

  • Faculty & Staff

    Your personal jedi masters

    Your primary instructor will be Avi Flombaum. He's taught over 300 people how to code and will be spending every waking moment making sure you can change the world with a keyboard. You'll also be learning from programmers at some of the best tech companies in New York through guest lectures.
