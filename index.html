  • Our Students & Alumni

    What graduates grok

    The Flatiron school is a 12 week, full time program, designed to turn you into a web developer. Prior to arriving to campus, you will complete 4 weeks (80-100 hours in total) of pre-work assignments at home. This, plus the following 12 weeks of immersive campus learning, amounts to a 16 week commitment.

  • Apply to Become a Student

    What we look for

    The goal of the program is to give you the equivalent skillset and experience of an entry-level Ruby developer. That means you'll be an internet swiss-army knife, equipped with all the tools needed to build applications. You don't need any programming experience to attend, just a strong desire to create and a whole lot of grit.
